Hick's Law

Fitt's Law

Spotify

spotify.com

In spotify page there is something interesting. Also we have many information to navigate but they have created a way in wich you have all the options in just the first page. You will not waste time searching the information that you want navigating in menus and many submenus. Yo have almost all wat you want in the home page and if you want find something yo will do it fast. For that reason I chose spotify as an example of Fitt's Law.
